Project # 2 - Analyzing Commercial Truck data



Case Study description:

The Marketing Director has asked me to assess the state of the Refuse Market and make recommendations for the company to pursue growth opportunities.

The Director has also provided some questions to answer in the process:

  • Who would you consult for guidance?
  • Which OEMs are you most concerned about?
  • What would you expect for 2020 (hypothetically w/o the pandemic)?
  • What opportunities do you see in this market (fuel types, vehicle configurations, etc)?
  • Is there something that characterizes a “refuse” customer?
  • Would you recommend research or surveys?

    Recommendations to Management:

    1. Cummmins, Mack, and Paccar are the OEMs to be concerned about, with emphasis on Paccar
    2. CNG is the best solution for now before Electric changeover
    3. Focus on smaller engine displacements = Customers won't pay for more gas usage
    4. Focus on Small and Medium Fleet customers, as they have more predictable purchasing patterns
